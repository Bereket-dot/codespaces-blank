About the Game 🧩

This is a classic sliding puzzle game where the objective is to arrange the tiles in numerical order. The game gets more challenging with higher difficulty levels.

×

Instructions 📜

1. Select the difficulty level (3x3, 4x4, or 5x5).

2. Click on a numbered tile to move it into the empty space.

3. Solve the puzzle by arranging the tiles in ascending order.
